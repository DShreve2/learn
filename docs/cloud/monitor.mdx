Netdata Cloud gives you the ability to monitor the health and performance of an entire infrastructure from a single
interface. Using Cloud's connected features, you can view real-time metrics from all your nodes, troubleshoot anomalies
using embedded dashboards, and track issues with alarms and notifications.

View more about each of Netdata Cloud's monitoring features below.

<Discover>
  <DiscoverBox 
    href="/docs/cloud/monitor/nodes" 
    title="View all nodes at a glance">
    See charts from all your nodes in one pane of glass, then dive in to embedded dashboards for granular troubleshooting of ongoing issues.
  </DiscoverBox>
  <DiscoverBox 
    href="/docs/cloud/monitor/alarms" 
    title="Audit active alarms">
    Track the health of your infrastructure in one place by taking advantage of the powerful health monitoring watchdog running on every node.
  </DiscoverBox>
  <DiscoverBox 
    href="/docs/cloud/monitor/notifications" 
    title="Enable alarm notifications">
    Configure Cloud to send notifications to your team whenever any node on your infrastructure triggers an alarm.
  </DiscoverBox>
</Discover>
